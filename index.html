Published research

Sustainable Food Supply Chain Optimization under Uncertainty

Two published multi-objective optimization studies designing sustainable, resilient agri-food supply chains under uncertainty: a real Ontario wheat network (farms, elevators, DCs, demand zones) with intermodal transport and winter-disruption effects, and the Canadian soybean export chain (to China, Europe, and the USA) ranking suppliers across 20 sustainability criteria over 18 demand/price scenarios.

Multi-Objective Optimization Two-Stage Stochastic Fuzzy Robust Best-Worst Method IBM CPLEX
Result
Wheat: intermodal transport cut costs 8% and delivery times 20% vs. truck-only. Soybean: SDG-aligned incentives offset a 12% profit gap, making sustainable sourcing viable.
Journal of Cleaner Production (IF 9.8) & Sustainable Production and Consumption (IF 12.1)
Machine learning

Minority Class Detection in Diabetic Diagnosis

ML pipeline for multiclass diabetes prediction (healthy / prediabetic / diabetic) using the CDC BRFSS dataset (253,680 records, 21 features, 84.2% class imbalance). Evaluated 6 resampling strategies across 5 classifiers; applied Borda count consensus ranking for robust feature selection.

Optuna SMOTE / Tomek Links LightGBM CatBoost Python
Result
Tomek Links + LightGBM/CatBoost achieved best performance; 15% improvement in recall for minority classes, prioritizing low false-negatives for clinical use.
Working paper - under review (2026)
Data mining · Open source

Conversion Pattern Mining for Digital Marketing Campaigns

End-to-end data mining project on 8,000 customer records identifying the behaviors and campaign traits that drive conversions. Full KDD pipeline, EDA, three classifiers (Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest), and an Apriori association-rule miner implemented from scratch; handled an 87.6% imbalanced target with class weighting and evaluated on lift and ROC-AUC.

Python scikit-learn Random Forest Apriori (from scratch) pandas
Result
Random Forest reached 0.90 accuracy and 0.81 ROC-AUC; behavioral signals (time on site, pages/visit, CTR, ad spend) far outweighed demographics, with high-spend Conversion campaigns confirmed at 95-96% confidence.
