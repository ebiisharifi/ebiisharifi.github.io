04 / Research & Projects

Selected projects.

Optimization, predictive modeling, and decision support - applied end-to-end.

PROJECT / 01

Sustainable Food Supply Chain Optimization under Uncertainty

Two published multi-objective optimization studies designing sustainable, resilient agri-food supply chains under uncertainty: a real Ontario wheat network (farms, elevators, DCs, demand zones) with intermodal transport and winter-disruption effects, and the Canadian soybean export chain (→ China / Europe / USA) ranking suppliers across 20 sustainability criteria over 18 demand/price scenarios.

Result: Wheat — intermodal transport cut costs 8% and delivery times 20% vs. truck-only. Soybean — SDG-aligned incentives offset a 12% profit gap, making sustainable sourcing viable.
Multi-Objective Optimization Two-Stage Stochastic Fuzzy Robust Best-Worst Method IBM CPLEX
Published in Journal of Cleaner Production (IF 9.8) & Sustainable Production and Consumption (IF 12.1)
PROJECT / 02

Minority Class Detection in Diabetic Diagnosis

ML pipeline for multiclass diabetes prediction (healthy / prediabetic / diabetic) using the CDC BRFSS dataset (253,680 records, 21 features, 84.2% class imbalance). Evaluated 6 resampling strategies across 5 classifiers; applied Borda count consensus ranking for robust feature selection.

Result: Tomek Links + LightGBM/CatBoost achieved best performance; 15% improvement in recall for minority classes, prioritizing low false-negatives for clinical use.
Optuna SMOTE / Tomek Links LightGBM CatBoost Python
Working paper - under review (2026)
PROJECT / 03

Conversion Pattern Mining for Digital Marketing Campaigns

End-to-end data mining project on 8,000 customer records identifying the behaviors and campaign traits that drive conversions. Full KDD pipeline — EDA, three classifiers (Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest), and an Apriori association-rule miner implemented from scratch; handled an 87.6% imbalanced target with class weighting and evaluated on lift and ROC-AUC.

Result: Random Forest reached 0.90 accuracy and 0.81 ROC-AUC; behavioral signals (time on site, pages/visit, CTR, ad spend) far outweighed demographics, with high-spend Conversion campaigns confirmed at 95-96% confidence.
Python scikit-learn Random Forest Apriori (from scratch) pandas
